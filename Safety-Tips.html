Safety First

Fireworks Safety Guidelines

Follow these essential safety tips to ensure a joyful and secure celebration for everyone

Children Safety

Adult supervision always required
Keep children at safe distance (10+ feet)
Never let children handle crackers alone
Don't allow picking up unexploded items
65% of injuries involve children

Home & Property Safety

Use in open areas only
Keep water bucket nearby
Never use indoors or near buildings
Don't ignite near vehicles or trees
30% of fires caused by improper use

Personal Protection

Wear safety glasses and gloves
Use long lighters or incense sticks
Never hold crackers in your hands
Don't wear loose or synthetic clothing
45% of injuries are hand/eye related

Legal & Quality

Buy from licensed dealers only
Check quality certificates
Don't buy illegal or uncertified products
Avoid homemade or modified crackers
100% legal compliance at SK Crackers

Environmental Safety

Choose eco-friendly green crackers
Follow local time restrictions
Don't burst during high pollution days
Avoid areas with elderly/sick people
70% less pollution with green crackers

Emergency Preparedness

Keep first aid kit ready
Know emergency contact numbers
Don't panic during emergencies
Never use water on chemical fires
911 Emergency | 101 Fire
